Welcome to Peernote

Peernote is an educational platform that connects writers and instructors, providing tools to create and collaboratively review papers.

A Tool for Writers

Upgrade the Way You Write

Write Wherever, Whenever

Peernote provides a full-fledged word processor accessible entirely from the browser. Already started a paper? Upload it! Peernote supports a wide variety of document formats.

Never Write Between the Lines Again

With Peernote's comprehensive in-browser editor, you can edit essays with ease. You can utilize our suite of grammar, spelling, and plagiarism-checking tools to keep your writing clean.

What Goes Around Comes Around

Provide a review, get one back! Our platform connects you with other writers who can provide feedback on your work in exchange for your feedback on theirs.

A Tool for Instructors

Bringing technology to the classroom

Streamline Instruction

With Peernote, the entire writing process is contained within the application, including assigning work, writing papers, peer reviewing essays, and grading. You can spend more time instructing and less time doing busywork.

Classroom Analytics

Peernote helps focus instruction by providing a detailed analytical breakdown of classroom and individual writing proficiency. You can identify strengths and weaknesses in grammar or writing fluency or pinpoint plagiarism violations.

Standardized Reviews

Instructors and students both have access to Peernote's document markup editor. Avoid issues of legibility, or confusion over the various editing symbols and idiosyncrasies.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the Peernote beta open?

We will be launching our private beta for users in March. Our instructor beta will open in May. Sign up here to receive a notification when the beta opens.

Which features can I expect in the initial beta?

You will initially be able to write, open and save documents in our editor, upload word documents, and send your essay for review to friends via email. Reviewers will be able to use our document markup editor.

Which features will roll out after beta launch?

After the beta opens, we will be rapidly adding features. These include improved review tools for grammar analysis and plagiarism checking, richer document text, and automatic peer matching for essay reviews.

How much will Peernote cost me?

If you are a student or writer, Peernote is free now and forever. If you are a school administrator, please contact us for quotes.

Why are we building Peernote?

The literature on effective writing education centers on providing numerous rich writing experiences like editing, revising, and learning from mistakes. Peernote allows students to write and edit collaboratively, providing easy and efficient access to these crucial writing experiences.

I have Google Docs. Why Peernote?

Peernote streamlines the processes that take place in class everyday - distributing assignments, writing, editing, and grading all in the same place. Additionally, instructors gain powerful insights into how their students write and how they can improve.

Who makes Peernote?

We're a dedicated team who met and founded Peernote at Brown University. Bryce is founder and front-end developer, Jackson and Alec are back-end engineers, Linh is our lovely designer, and Jeff takes care of business. Learn more about us here.

I have another question. Who can I ask?

Our founders are happy to answer your questions. Feel free to send us a message here. We don't bite! (Except for Jackson maybe).
