Hello from Peernote!

Technology has improved essentially every aspect of daily life, yet classrooms have lagged behind. Teachers still issue and collect paper handouts, they mark-up essays by hand, and they enter grades into outdated systems. Growing class sizes and dwindling educational funding has only made managing classrooms more difficult. Teachers spend less time on their students and more time on the busywork necessary to run a classroom smoothly.

That's where we come in. We're a small team trying to make a big difference. Peernote was founded at Brown University by a group of entrepreneurial-minded programmers and designers. We're passionate about the problem and we have the skills and the drive to solve it.
