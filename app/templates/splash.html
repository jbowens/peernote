Peernote is a platform that enables writers and instructors to connect and collaboratively review and edit written material.

A Tool for Writers

Upgrade the Way You Write.

Write Wherever, Whenever

Peernote provides a full-fledged word processor accessible entirely from the browser. Already started a paper? Upload it! Peernote supports a wide variety of document formats.

Never Write Between the Lines Again

With Peernote's comprehensive in-browser editor, you can edit essays with ease. You can utilize our suite of grammar, spelling, and plagiarism-checking tools to keep your writing clean.

What Goes Around Comes Around

Provide a review, get one back! Our platform connects you with other writers who can provide feedback on your work in exchange for your feedback on theirs.

A Tool for Instructors

Bringing technology to the classroom

Streamline Instruction

With Peernote, the entire writing process is contained within the application, including assigning work, writing papers, peer reviewing essays, and grading. You can spend more time instructing and less time rummaging through student papers, entering grades, and printing assignments.

Classroom Analytics

Peernote helps focus instruction by providing a detailed analytical breakdown of classroom and individual writing proficiency. You can identify strengths and weaknesses in grammar or writing fluency or pinpoint plagiarism violations.

Standardized Reviews

Instructors and students both have access to Peernote's document markup editor. Never again will there be issues of legibility, or confusion over the various editing symbols and idiosyncrasies.
